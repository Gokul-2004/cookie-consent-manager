How It Works

When you visit this website, a cookie consent banner appears at the bottom of the page. This banner is DPDP compliant and gives you full control over which cookies you accept.

You can choose to:

  • Accept All: Accept all cookies including analytics and marketing
  • Reject All: Only accept essential cookies (required for website functionality)
  • Customize: Choose exactly which cookie categories you want to allow

Your preferences are saved and you can change them anytime by clicking the cookie settings link (usually in the footer) or by using the widget's preference center.
